Projects Here are some of the more notable projects that I've done/participated in that have not been lost to time, they are in descending chronological order.

Company Issue & Suggestion System - Voice of Employees

A software to vastly improve the communication and tracking of the connected issues and possible improvement inside a company. The core idea of the project is to allow easy communication between the different employees of a company which is often made difficult due to pen and paper solutions or interpersonal communication problems. Voice of Employees won the Lean Institute Bulgaria's 2023 Operational Excellence Award for Employee Engagement. Here is a photo of me accepting our reward and giving a speech.
Trailer / Presentation

Augmented Reality Greek God Defense Game

We made the project for the football club Herakles Almelo as they wanted a half-time activity to keep their fans engaged. Inspired by their mascot Herakles - we made a Greek God themed tower defense game in augmented reality. The idea is for every fan sitting in the stadium seats to be able to enjoy some quick action-packed entertainment while they are waiting for the football match to resume. This project concluded here since it was happening in the midst of the pandemic and the focus of the client had to shift entirely.

Hack Vratsa 2016 Winning Project - Browser Adventure Game

Together with a group of friends from school we decided to participate in our first ever Hackathon. We made an adventure game with different characters and scenarios that can be played in the browser. To our amazement we ended up winning first place and were awarded with a visit to the European Parliament in Brussels.
